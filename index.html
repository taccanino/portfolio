About Me

I am Pietro Brega, a technology and IT enthusiast who has been pursuing his passion since childhood through studies and personal initiatives. I graduated from Majorana-Giorgi School in Genoa with a specialization in computer science, achieving a score of 97/100 in 2018.

During my academic journey, I participated in four training internships at prestigious organizations including Liguria Digitale, the Italian Institute of Technology, and the Industrial Experts College of Genoa. Between 2019 and 2020, I gained valuable work experience developing web applications for private clients using PHP, JavaScript, HTML, and MySQL.

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Genoa in 2023 with the highest honors (110/110). Throughout my studies, I worked on various university and private projects using multiple programming languages and frameworks including React, Typescript, C#, Python, PHP, PostgreSQL and MSSQL.

Currently, I work as a Software Team Leader at Leonardo SpA, where I lead the development of baggage handling systems for airports, including projects at Charles de Gaulle while supporting Geneva and Zurich airports.
